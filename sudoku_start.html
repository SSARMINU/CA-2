Instructions

Welcome to our website! Here are some instructions:

  1. Objective: Fill a 9x9 grid with numbers from 1 to 9, ensuring each row, column, and 3x3 subgrid contains all digits without repetition.
  2. Starting Grid: Begin with some cells pre-filled; these numbers cannot be changed.
  3. Rules: Each row, column, and subgrid must contain all numbers 1-9 without repeating any.
  4. How to Play: Use logic to deduce the correct numbers for each empty cell, starting with obvious placements and progressing methodically.
  5. Enjoy: Sudoku is a challenging game of logic and deduction; take your time and have fun solving the puzzle!
